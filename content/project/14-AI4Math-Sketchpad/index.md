## AI4Math - Sketchpad Project

Received grant from **AI for Math Fund** to develop the Sketchpad system for formal mathematics.

- Automatically converts mathematical proofs into structured data using graph-based representation
- Enables decomposition of proofs into individual statements for more precise auto-formalization
